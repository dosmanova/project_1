About Our Planets

Our Solar System has eight planets that orbit the Sun. Each one is unique:

  • Mercury - the smallest planet and closest to the Sun, with extreme temperatures.
  • Venus - similar in size to Earth, but covered with thick clouds and very hot.
  • Earth - our home, the only known planet with life.
  • Mars - the Red Planet, with dusty landscapes and signs of ancient water.
  • Jupiter - the largest planet, a gas giant with a Great Red Spot storm.
  • Saturn - famous for its beautiful rings made of ice and rock.
  • Uranus - a cold, tilted planet with faint rings and a bluish color.
  • Neptune - the farthest planet, known for strong winds and deep blue color.
